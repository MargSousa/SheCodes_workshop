MUSIC OF THE MONTH

Mumford and Sons are back!

If I had to choose my music of choice for this month it would definitely be Mumford and Sons. The british band that started in 2007 is led by Marcus Mumford and are known by their folky music with a banjo always present. Some of their most known songs are Little Lion Man, The Cave and I Will Wait. However, for the last years they had a change in sound and tuned it up by introducing more electric guitars into their songs. I myself, really enjoyed this change because they still maintain that Mumford and Sons vibe that we all know and love with a little rock n'roll. And now guess what? They are back with new music and a new tour which includes a date in Lisboa!

The band is returning to their folky roots with the release of a new album titled Delta, which includes the new single Guiding Light. This will be a groundbreaking new show not to be missed! If you would like to join this event, tickets are already on sale and you can purchase them below.
